Types of Sentences

Simple Sentence ✨

A sentence with a single independent clause.

Example: The sun is shining.

Compound Sentence 🔗

Two or more independent clauses joined by a conjunction.

Example: I wanted to play, but it started raining.

Complex Sentence 🌀

An independent clause and one or more dependent clauses.

Example: Although it was late, he continued working.

Declarative Sentence 📘

Make a statement or provide information.

Example: I enjoy reading books.

Interrogative Sentence ❓

Ask a question.

Example: What is your favorite hobby?

Imperative Sentence 🚦

Give commands or make requests.

Example: Please sit down.

More Examples

  • Simple Sentence: The dog barked. 🐕
  • Compound Sentence: She wanted to study, so she turned off the TV. 📺
  • Complex Sentence: If you work hard, you will succeed. 💪
  • Declarative Sentence: The earth revolves around the sun. 🌍
  • Interrogative Sentence: How do you solve this problem? 🤔
  • Imperative Sentence: Don't forget to submit your assignment. 📑
