Travel English

Learn how to communicate effectively while traveling. This module covers vocabulary, useful phrases, and sample dialogues for various travel situations.

Key Vocabulary

  • Ticket: A pass or document needed for travel (e.g., flight ticket, train ticket).
  • Passport: An official document needed to travel internationally.
  • Luggage: Bags or suitcases used to carry personal belongings.
  • Departure: The act of leaving a place, typically used for flights and trains.
  • Arrival: Reaching a destination after traveling.

Sample Dialogues

Ticket Agent: Sure! What time would you like to depart?

Useful Travel Phrases

  • Where is the nearest airport?
  • How do I get to the train station?
  • Can I have the bill, please?
  • What time does the flight depart?
