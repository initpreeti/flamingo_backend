Understanding Articles

What are Articles?

Articles are words that define a noun as specific or unspecific. The two types of articles are definite articles and indefinite articles.

Definite Article: The

The is used to refer to something specific or unique.

Example: The sun rises in the east.

Indefinite Articles: A, An

A and An are used to refer to non-specific nouns.

  • A: Used before words starting with consonant sounds. Example: A car, A book
  • An: Used before words starting with vowel sounds. Example: An apple, An elephant

When Not to Use Articles

Articles are not used with plural or uncountable nouns when talking generally.

  • Example: Cats are friendly animals.
  • Example: I love music.
