Dining at a Restaurant

Learn how to order food, interact with restaurant staff, and more in English. This module covers key phrases, vocabulary, and dialogues for dining out.

Key Vocabulary

  • Menu: A list of food and drinks offered by the restaurant.
  • Waiter/Waitress: The person who serves food and drinks.
  • Order: To request food or drink.
  • Bill/Check: The total amount to be paid for the meal.
  • Reservation: Booking a table in advance.

Sample Dialogues

Customer: Can I see the menu, please?

Waiter: Of course! Here is the menu. What would you like to order?

Customer: I would like to have the grilled chicken and a glass of water.

Waiter: Sure! Would you like any appetizers with that?

Interactive Practice

Question: What would you say if you wanted to ask for the bill?
