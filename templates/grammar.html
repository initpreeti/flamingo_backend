Basic Grammar 📘

Learn the essential rules of English grammar to form correct sentences.

Nouns and Pronouns 🔊

A noun is a word that represents a person, place, thing, or idea. Examples: *dog*, *city*, *happiness*.

A pronoun is used in place of a noun. Examples: *he*, *she*, *it*.

Verbs ✍️

A verb is an action word. Examples: *run*, *eat*, *study*. Learn how to use verbs in sentences.

Adjectives 🔆

Adjectives describe nouns. Examples: *beautiful*, *large*, *red*. Learn how to use adjectives effectively.

Sentence Structure 📑

Learn how to form sentences using the basic structure: Subject + Verb + Object.
