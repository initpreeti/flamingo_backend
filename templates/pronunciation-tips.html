Pronunciation Tips

1. Focus on the stress pattern of words to sound more natural.

2. Pay attention to vowel sounds, as they can change the meaning of a word.

3. Practice speaking slowly to improve clarity.

4. Listen to native speakers to understand the correct rhythm and intonation.

5. Break words into smaller syllables to make pronunciation easier.

6. Practice tongue twisters to improve articulation.

7. Pay attention to silent letters, which may not be pronounced but affect spelling.

8. Use stress to distinguish between noun-verb pairs, e.g., "record" (noun) vs. "record" (verb).

9. Practice minimal pairs (words that differ by only one sound) to improve accuracy.

10. Make use of online dictionaries that include pronunciation guides.

11. Use your hands to emphasize stress and rhythm in speech.

12. Record yourself speaking and compare it to native speakers for improvement.
