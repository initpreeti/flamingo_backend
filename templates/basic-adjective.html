What is an Adjective?

An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun by providing information about its quality, quantity, number, or state. Adjectives make nouns or pronouns more specific and detailed.

Types of Adjectives

  • 1.Descriptive Adjectives:
    Describe the quality of a noun.
    Example: *Beautiful* house.
    This pen is mine.
    Those flowers are beautiful.
  • 2.Quantitative Adjectives:
    Indicate the quantity.
    Example: *Five* apples.
  • 3.Demonstrative Adjectives:
    Point out specific things.
    Example: *This* book, *that* car.
  • 4.Possessive Adjectives:
    Show possession.
    Example: *My* pen, *your* bag.
  • 5.Interrogative Adjectives:
    Used in questions.
    Example: *Which* color do you prefer?
