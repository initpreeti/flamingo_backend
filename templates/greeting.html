Lesson 1: Basic Greetings

  • "Hello!" - Pronunciation: /həˈloʊ/
  • "Good Morning!" - Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/
  • "How are you?" - Pronunciation: /haʊ ɑːr juː/
  • "Good Afternoon!" - Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˌɑːftərˈnuːn/
  • "Good Evening!" - Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˈiːvnɪŋ/
  • "I'm fine, thank you!" - Pronunciation: /aɪm faɪn, θæŋk juː/
  • "What’s up?" - Pronunciation: /wɒts ʌp/
  • "Good night!" - Pronunciation: /ɡʊd naɪt/
  • "Good to see you!" - Pronunciation: /ɡʊd tə siː juː/
  • "How’s it going?" - Pronunciation: /haʊz ɪt ˈɡəʊɪŋ/
  • "See you later!" - Pronunciation: /siː juː ˈleɪtər/
  • "Take care!" - Pronunciation: /teɪk keər/
  • "Hi, how are you doing?" - Pronunciation: /haɪ, haʊ ɑːr juː ˈduːɪŋ/
  • "What’s new?" - Pronunciation: /wɒts njuː/
  • "It’s been a while!" - Pronunciation: /ɪts bɪn ə waɪl/
  • "Long time no see!" - Pronunciation: /lɒŋ taɪm nəʊ siː/

Lesson 2: Introducing Yourself

  • "My name is [Your Name]." - Pronunciation: /maɪ neɪm ɪz/
  • "Nice to meet you!" - Pronunciation: /naɪs tə miːt juː/
  • "I am from [Your Country]." - Pronunciation: /aɪ æm frəm/
  • "I live in [City Name]." - Pronunciation: /aɪ lɪv ɪn/
  • "I’m a student." - Pronunciation: /aɪm ə ˈstjuːdənt/
  • "I work as a [Your Job]." - Pronunciation: /aɪ wɜːrk æz ə/
  • "I’m a teacher." - Pronunciation: /aɪm ə ˈtiːtʃər/
  • "I’m learning English." - Pronunciation: /aɪm ˈlɜːnɪŋ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ/
  • "I’m here for vacation." - Pronunciation: /aɪm hɪər fɔːr veɪˈkeɪʃən/
  • "I like traveling." - Pronunciation: /aɪ laɪk ˈtrævəlɪŋ/
  • "This is my friend [Friend's Name]." - Pronunciation: /ðɪs ɪz maɪ frɛnd/
  • "I am studying [Your Major]." - Pronunciation: /aɪ æm ˈstʌdɪɪŋ/
  • "I enjoy cooking." - Pronunciation: /aɪ ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ˈkʊkɪŋ/

Lesson 3: Asking Questions

  • "What is your name?" - Pronunciation: /wɒt ɪz jɔːr neɪm/
  • "Where are you from?" - Pronunciation: /wɛər ɑːr juː frʌm/
  • "How old are you?" - Pronunciation: /haʊ oʊld ɑːr juː/
  • "Where do you live?" - Pronunciation: /wɛər dʊ juː lɪv/
  • "What do you do?" - Pronunciation: /wɒt dʊ juː duː/
  • "Do you speak English?" - Pronunciation: /duː juː spiːk ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ/
  • "What’s your favorite color?" - Pronunciation: /wɒts jɔːr ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈkʌlər/
  • "When is your birthday?" - Pronunciation: /wɛn ɪz jɔːr ˈbɜːrθdeɪ/
  • "What’s the time?" - Pronunciation: /wɒts ðə taɪm/
  • "Where can I find a restaurant?" - Pronunciation: /wɛər kæn aɪ faɪnd ə ˈrɛstrɒnt/
  • "How much does this cost?" - Pronunciation: /haʊ mʌʧ dʌz ðɪs kɒst/
