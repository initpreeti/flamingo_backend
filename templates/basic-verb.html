📘 What is a Verb?

A verb is a word that represents an action, occurrence, or state of being. Verbs are essential in sentences as they describe what the subject is doing.

Example: She runs every morning. (Action)

📚 Types of Verbs

1️⃣ Action Verbs

These verbs describe physical or mental actions.

Examples: Run, Jump, Think, Write.

2️⃣ Linking Verbs

These verbs connect the subject to additional information.

Examples: Is, Am, Are, Become.

3️⃣ Auxiliary Verbs

Also known as helping verbs, they assist the main verb.

Examples: Can, Will, Should, Have.

4️⃣ Transitive and Intransitive Verbs

Transitive verbs need a direct object. Example: She ate an apple. Intransitive verbs do not need a direct object. Example: He sleeps.
